## 1) One-liner
Describe the product in one sentence.

## 2) Target user
Who is this for? What job are they hiring it to do?

## 3) Problem
What pain exists today? How do users solve it now?

## 4) Outcome
What measurable improvement should a user experience?

## 5) Constraints
Hard constraints (budget, timelines, platforms, compliance, mobile-first, etc.).

## 6) Non-goals
Explicitly state what is out of scope for v1.

## 7) Success metrics
2–5 metrics (activation, retention, conversion, latency, support volume, etc.).

## 8) Risks
Top risks (technical, legal, product, go-to-market) and mitigations.

## 9) Open questions
Add questions that need answers before building.
