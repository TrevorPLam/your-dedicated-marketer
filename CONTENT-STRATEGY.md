### On-Page SEO Checklist
- [ ] Unique, descriptive title tags (50-60 characters)
- [ ] Compelling meta descriptions (150-160 characters)
- [ ] H1 on every page matching user intent
- [ ] Internal linking between related pages
- [ ] Image alt text for accessibility and SEO
- [ ] Schema markup (Organization, Service, FAQPage)
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design
- [ ] Fast page load times (<2s)

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Strategic**: Data-informed, business-focused
- **Approachable**: Jargon-free, conversational
- **Confident**: Expertise without arrogance
- **Results-focused**: Outcomes over vanity metrics

### Tone Variations

**Homepage/Services**: Professional, confident, value-focused
**Blog**: Educational, helpful, conversational
**Case Studies**: Results-driven, specific, credible
**About**: Authentic, passionate, relatable

### Writing Guidelines
- Use "you" to address reader directly
- Active voice over passive
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for scannability
- Avoid marketing jargon
- Back claims with data or examples
