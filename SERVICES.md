## Engagement Models

### Retainer (Monthly)
- Ongoing services with consistent deliverables
- Predictable monthly investment
- Priority support and communication
- Most common for Core and Support services

### Project-Based
- Defined scope and deliverables
- Fixed timeline and budget
- Best for Add-Ons and one-time initiatives
- Examples: Website redesigns, SEO audits, funnel builds

### Advisory (Quarterly)
- Strategic guidance and planning
- Quarterly reviews and planning sessions
- Async communication between sessions
- Best for businesses with in-house marketing teams

## Getting Started

1. **Discovery Call**: 30-minute conversation to understand your business and goals
2. **Marketing Audit**: Review current marketing efforts and identify opportunities
3. **Strategy Proposal**: Custom plan with recommended services and timeline
4. **Onboarding**: Kickoff meeting, access setup, and initial deliverables planning
5. **Execution**: Regular delivery of services with ongoing communication and optimization
